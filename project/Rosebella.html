Delivery Policy

Same-Day Delivery

Orders placed before 3:00 PM qualify for same-day delivery. Orders after 3 PM will be delivered the following day or on your selected date.

Delivery Areas

We deliver across all of Doha, Qatar including West Bay, The Pearl, Lusail, Al Wakrah, and surrounding areas.

Delivery Times

Available slots: 9 AM–12 PM · 12–3 PM · 3–6 PM · 6–9 PM. Select your preferred slot at checkout.

Cancellations & Returns

Cancellations accepted up to 2 hours before scheduled delivery. Due to the perishable nature of our products, we do not accept returns once delivered.
